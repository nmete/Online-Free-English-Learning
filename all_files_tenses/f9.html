FUTURE PERFECT TENSE



The future perfect is a verb tense used for actions that will be completed before some other point in the future.

General formula or structure :- will have + [past participle].


Following chart represents examples of future perfect tense in different forms.

Affirmative Examples Negative Examples Interogative Examples

You will have decided. You won't have decided. Will you have decided?
