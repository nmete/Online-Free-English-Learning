PAST PERFECT TENSE



The past perfect, also called the pluperfect, is a verb tense used to talk about actions that were completed before some point in the past.

General formula or structure :- had + [past participle]


Following chart represents examples of past perfect tense in different forms.

Affirmative Examples Negative Examples Interogative Examples
I had decided. I hadn't decided. had I decided?
You had decided. You hadn't decided. had you decided?
